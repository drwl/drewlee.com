Greg Bauges: Developers and Depression (Talk)

I stumbled across this sometime in late 2013. At the time, I didn’t know much about mental health, but I always felt that there was something off within me when it came to productivity and procrastination.

Hearing about Greg’s journey and his experiences resonated with me, and it made an impact on my life.

Just give a watch, and if it doesn’t resonate with you, perhaps it will shed light on what others may be facing and going through.
